ABOUT US

Fighting for equal rights to health and well-being for vulnerable children and women in Uganda.
Partnering with local and international charity organisations for impact

Divine Life Transforming Centre International (DLTC International) is a Christian relief, development and advocacy organisation that has existed for several years in practice but officially registered as an organisation in 2023.

The organisation focuses on the welfare and the rights of vulnerable women and children in Uganda.

DLTC envisions a society where women and children’s rights are respected and exists to promote a coordinated action for the promotion and protection of the rights of children and women according to the

Universal Declaration of Human Rights as  adopted by the UN General Assembly on 10 December 1948.

    The focus of the work of “Divine Life Transforming Center International (DLTC)" is mainly on:
  • Humanitarian aid for people in need of food and material support
  • Medical assistance for the sick people
  • Care and sponsorships of orphans
  • Socio-educational projects and initiatives for children, young people and families.

Our principles: Humanity - Honesty - Transparency- Development Partnership
